    The Theme

    This year's Orientation theme is a scavenger hunt! Meaning members will have the chance to meet and work together with other members as treasure hunters to find objectives around campus!

    This provides an opporunity for fresh new students to explore and familiarize themselves with the University of Toronto St. George Campus!
